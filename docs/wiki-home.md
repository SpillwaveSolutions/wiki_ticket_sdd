# WikiTicket SDD

WikiTicket SDD (pronounced "wicked ticket") is spec-driven development with
visible WIP: every plan becomes tracked work items in a git-native event log,
the roadmap is generated from those items, and everything — plans, roadmap,
history — is published to the wiki and tracker your team already uses. AI
work is fishbowled, not hidden.
